Double Integral Trapezoidal

Approximates ∬ f(x,y) dA over a rectangular region [a,b] × [c,d] using the repeated trapezoidal rule.

Formula
∬f(x,y) dA ≈ (hₓ·hᵧ) · Σᵢ Σⱼ wᵢⱼ · f(xᵢ, yⱼ)
wᵢⱼ = (½ if endpoint, 1 if interior) for each axis
hₓ = (b−a)/m    hᵧ = (d−c)/n
∬ Function & Integration Region
Supports: x, y, +, −, *, /, ^, sin, cos, tan, exp, ln, sqrt, abs, pi
x bounds [a, b]
y bounds [c, d]

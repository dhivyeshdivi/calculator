Trapezoidal Rule

Approximate ∫ f(x) dx over [a, b] by summing trapezoidal areas under the curve.

Formula
I ≈ (h/2) · [ f(x₀) + 2(f(x₁) + … + f(xₙ₋₁)) + f(xₙ) ]
where h = (b − a) / n
∫ Function & Limits
Supports: x, +, −, *, /, ^, sin, cos, tan, exp, ln, sqrt, abs, pi
More → higher accuracy
Known Examples
∫₀¹ x² dx = 1/3≈0.3333 → x^2, 0→1
∫₀ᵖⁱ sin(x) dx = 2 → sin(x), 0→pi
∫₀¹ eˣ dx ≈ 1.7183 → exp(x), 0→1

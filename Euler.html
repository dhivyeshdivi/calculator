Euler Method

Forward Euler approximation for first-order ODEs: dy/dx = f(x, y).

Formula
yₙ₊₁ = yₙ + h · f(xₙ, yₙ)
xₙ₊₁ = xₙ + h   (advance x by step size)
∂ Function & Parameters
